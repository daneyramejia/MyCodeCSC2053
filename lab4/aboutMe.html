Thank you for coming to the About Me tab!

My name is Daneyra Mejia. I am originally from The Bronx, NYC, however I am currently residing on Villanova's campus.

Biography:

I am currently a sophomore at Villanova University's College of Liberal Arts and Science as a Computer Science major and double minor in Spanish and Communications. I am a Peer Leader in CASA, a UNITAS Leader, the Publicity Chair of Villanovans in Defense of Animals, and an Assistant to the VP of Fundraising in the Villanova Band. Before coming to campus, I was a founding member of the UpBeat NYC Orchestra and Jazz Band, along with playing in 2 national orchestras and an international orchestra. In June of 2019, I graduated third in my class at The Bronx School for Law, Government, and Justice.
